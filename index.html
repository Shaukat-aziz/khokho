Rules

Summary of the club's Kho-Kho rules (detailed PDF available below).

Match format: Two teams (typically 12 in squad; 9 field players) play two innings each. Matches are time-based with chasing/defending turns.

Field & players: Standard Kho-Kho rectangular court with marked poles. Players rotate positions; substitutes allowed as per event rules.

Scoring & dismissals: Chasers score by touching/tagging defenders according to the rulebook; defenders are dismissed when tagged or when violating lane/position rules. Points are awarded per dismissal and for team performance during the innings.

Fouls & conduct: Illegal contact, unsportsmanlike behavior, deliberate obstruction, or unsafe play result in penalties. Respect, safety, and fair play are mandatory.

Session rules: Club sessions follow a simplified, practice-friendly variant of tournament rules; referees/coaches will clarify interpretations during events.
